What is the HighLevel Google Sheets integration?

The HighLevel Google Sheets integration by LC Bridge keeps a live, two-way sync between the HighLevel (GoHighLevel) CRM and a Google Sheet. Update a contact or pipeline stage in HighLevel and the sheet updates automatically; edit a row in the sheet and HighLevel catches up. A spreadsheet change can even trigger a HighLevel workflow. It installs as a white-label add-on inside your sub-account — no code required.

Questions

HighLevel Google Sheets integration FAQ

What teams ask before connecting.

Can I connect HighLevel (GoHighLevel) to Google Sheets?
Yes. LC Bridge keeps a two-way sync between your HighLevel contacts and records and a Google Sheet, so both stay up to date automatically.
Is the sync two-way?
Yes. Changes in HighLevel update the sheet, and edits in the sheet flow back to HighLevel, in real time.
Do I need any code or Zapier?
No. Connect your Google account, choose a sheet, and map your fields. It's plug-and-play — no developer or third-party automation tool needed.
Can a spreadsheet change trigger a HighLevel workflow?
Yes. A new or updated row can start a HighLevel workflow, so your spreadsheet becomes a trigger, not just a report.
