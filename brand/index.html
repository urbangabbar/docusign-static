Brand system

Refined Cobalt.

The LC Bridge marketing identity. Confident, clean, technical — decisive cobalt over plenty of white, hierarchy by weight, zero gradient theatrics. Our product is a white-label chameleon that wears each agency's brand; this is the one place LC Bridge gets to look like itself.

Logo

The mark

The bridge mark — LC Bridge's namesake glyph — in the one sanctioned gradient, paired with the wordmark in Inter Tight. The gradient lives here and nowhere else.

LC Bridge
Rules

Always "LC Bridge" (two words). Full legal name "Lead Connector Bridge" only in footers, schema, and first mention.

Keep clear space ≥ the height of the mark. Never stretch, recolor the wordmark, or apply the gradient to anything but the mark.

Color

Palette

One brand color: cobalt. Violet is reserved exclusively for AI moments (and always labelled). Status hues are shared verbatim with the product so the marketing ties to the real UI.

Guardrails

Do / Don't

The point of Refined Cobalt is to not look like a templated AI site.

Do

  • Flat, decisive cobalt on generous white
  • Build hierarchy with weight & scale
  • Reserve violet strictly for labelled AI
  • Use the shared status hues for anything stateful
  • Real product visuals and honest numbers
  • Keep the one gradient on the logo mark only

Don't

  • Blue→violet gradients on heroes, headings, or buttons (the #1 AI tell)
  • A thick colored border down one side of a card
  • Generic indigo (#6366F1) or rainbow accents
  • Space Grotesk + Inter default combo
  • Emoji, Title Case, or hype adjectives
  • Glassmorphism / neon glow stacks
