1. Introduction & scope

SignBridge provides the DocuSign Integration (the "Service"), an integration that runs inside the HighLevel (GoHighLevel) CRM and connects your DocuSign account to your CRM so you can send agreements for signature, track signing status, and surface stalled agreements for follow-up.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Service collects, how we use and share it, how long we keep it, and the choices and rights you have. It applies to your use of the Service as installed within HighLevel and connected to your DocuSign account. It does not cover the separate practices of DocuSign or HighLevel, which are governed by their own privacy policies.

2. Information we collect

The Service collects and processes only the information needed to send agreements, keep their status current in your CRM, and operate securely. This includes:

  • HighLevel CRM data accessed — contact and opportunity data such as names, email addresses, and deal information, used to populate and send agreements.
  • DocuSign account information — your DocuSign account identifier, account base URI, and user id, used to call DocuSign on your behalf.
  • OAuth tokens — the access and refresh tokens issued when you connect your DocuSign account, stored encrypted and used to authenticate API calls.
  • Envelope status & metadata — signing status and related metadata synced back into your CRM so you can track progress.
  • Usage and log data — operational logs and diagnostic information generated as the Service runs, used to operate, secure, and troubleshoot the Service.

Key data points at a glance

  • HighLevel contact/opportunity data (names, emails, deal info)
  • DocuSign account id, base URI, and user id
  • Encrypted DocuSign OAuth tokens
  • Envelope status and metadata
  • Usage and log data

The Service does not store the contents of signed documents beyond the status information DocuSign provides, and it does not sell personal data.

3. How we use information

We use the information described above to deliver and maintain the Service. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Send agreements for signature from your CRM through your connected DocuSign account.
  • Sync envelope status back into the CRM so signing progress stays current.
  • Surface stalled or follow-up agreements so you can act on them.
  • Operate, secure, monitor, and troubleshoot the Service.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell it.

4. How information is shared

We share data only with the sub-processors necessary to deliver the Service:

  • DocuSign — to create and send envelopes and receive signing status (e-signature).
  • HighLevel / LeadConnector — the host CRM where the Service runs and where contact, opportunity, and status data live.
  • Our hosting and infrastructure providers — to run, store, and secure the Service.

We do not sell personal data. We may also disclose information where required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and the Service.

5. Data retention & deletion

We retain your DocuSign OAuth tokens only while the integration is connected. When you disconnect the integration or your account is deleted, the associated tokens are revoked and deleted.

You can disconnect at any time to revoke the Service's access to your DocuSign account. To request deletion of data we hold, contact us at support@leadconnectorbridge.com.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. OAuth tokens and secrets are encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM. Inbound DocuSign Connect webhooks are HMAC-verified before they are processed, so we only act on authentic events. We apply access controls to limit who and what can reach stored credentials and data.

7. Your rights & choices

You have control over the Service's access to your data. You can:

  • Access — request information about the data we hold related to your use of the Service.
  • Delete — request deletion of that data.
  • Disconnect — disconnect the integration at any time to revoke access and stop further processing.

To exercise any of these, contact support@leadconnectorbridge.com.

8. International users

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA, including the right to access, correct, or delete personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. We honor applicable rights regardless of location. You can exercise these rights by contacting support@leadconnectorbridge.com.

9. Children's privacy

The Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them. If you believe a child's data has been provided to us, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will note them here and update the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the Service after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, contact us at support@leadconnectorbridge.com.
